# Pricing & Plans

Incenta offers flexible pricing plans to match your needs. All plans include a 14-day free trial.

## Plans overview

| Plan | Monthly (USD) | Monthly (NPR) | API Limit | Max Apps |
|------|---------------|---------------|-----------|----------|
| Free | $0 | Rs 0 | 10,000 | 1 |
| Starter | $9 | Rs 1,199 | 25,000 | 3 |
| Growth | $29 | Rs 3,899 | 100,000 | 10 |
| Pro | $79 | Rs 10,599 | 500,000 | 25 |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Custom | Custom |

### Growth

### Enterprise

## Managing your subscription

View and manage your subscription from the **Billing** page in your dashboard.

- Upgrade or downgrade at any time
- Invoices are generated monthly
- API overages beyond your plan limit are billed at the plan's overage rate
- Cancel anytime — your plan remains active until the end of the billing period

## API endpoints

| Endpoint | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| `GET /partner/pricing-plans` | List available pricing plans |
| `GET /partner/billing` | Get billing info and usage |
| `POST /partner/subscription/upgrade` | Upgrade or change plan |
